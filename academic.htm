Library Courses

Related Media

Structuring an essay (powerpoint)

What did I do?

I attended a library course on Academic Writing, called Structuring an Essay

When did this take place?

I attended on the 14th of November

What have I gained from this activity?

I attended this course to help with my Academic Writing. The course was run by Angela Donaldson. Coming from a Web background, I'm not used to writing in this style and was struggling a little. This course was really helpful and I learned a lot about paraphrasing and essay layout. The slides were made available afterward which was really useful.

Library Learning Team

Related Media

Serious Games and VR report

What did I do?

I attended three Library Learning Team appointments

When did this take place?

I attended on the 12th, 20th and 27th of November

What have I gained from this activity?

I booked and attended a few appointments to get help with the report assignment for Essential Skills. I booked with Angela Donaldson mainly since i had spent time with her on the course and felt familiar. These sessions have been great because I was able to ask for specific help with areas I was struggling with and was given lots of additional advice on how to approach the task. This helped enormously with the report writing and I eventually managed to hand in something I was really happy with.

CERT Mentor Sessions

What did I do?

I attended four CERT Mentor sessions

When did this take place?

I attended on the 8th, 15th, 22nd and 29th of October

What have I gained from this activity?

These are a great opportunity to get information regarding tests and general tips. I feel that, in general, I don’t have a huge amount of questions that need answering so the usefulness is somewhat limited. However they have been extremely useful on occasion, especially regarding information on testing procedures from the mentor who has obviously been through them before.
