What did I do?

I attended the LGBT Leaders event in London

When did this take place?

The visit was on the 13th of October

What have I gained from this activity?

This was mainly aimed at a younger audience than me but there were some fun, informative industry sessions which turned up a few valuable points. I attended panels on Entrepreneurship, Tech Industry, PR/Media industry, attended the job fair for the sponsor companies and a Q&A regarding application strategies. Some good points were brought up and I feel it has made me think a little differently in terms of what sets people apart when applying.
